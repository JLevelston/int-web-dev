To the Future

I was an honor roll student througout my whole school year. I was a star athlete also. At this time I was still trying to find myself and the field I see myself going into. I later took Computer Science courses and found that challenging, but the more I did it, I got a little more comfortable. Weird right? That's how I found my passion in coding. At home I practice creating web pages and studying new codes. Taking this journey is like all the challenges I faced in life and I will take it head on!
